Project Description

Marco’s data are part of a longitudinal study consisting of 28 sessions of 45 min. each, collected between 1;05 and 2;05. The data have been collected and recorded by the mother during spontaneous mother-child interaction. Marco is the first-born child of a upper-middle class family living in the Northeast of Italy.
